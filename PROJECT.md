# QA Operating System

## Overview
QA Operating System is a long-term portfolio project that simulates the evolution of a software company from its first days to a mature engineering organization.

Its objective is not to showcase testing tools, but to demonstrate how a modern Quality Assurance system can be designed, implemented and evolved as a company grows.

> **QA Operating System is a living simulation of engineering quality, built to demonstrate not only what I know, but how I think, make decisions and evolve as a QA Leader.**

## Mission
Build a realistic simulation of how a Quality Assurance organization evolves alongside a growing software company.

## Vision
Create a living ecosystem rather than a static portfolio.

## Purpose
- QA Leadership
- QA Strategy
- Engineering Thinking
- Test Management
- Test Automation
- Release Management
- AI-assisted QA
- Risk Analysis
- Decision Making
- Continuous Improvement

## Audience
- Recruiters
- QA Managers
- Engineering Managers
- CTOs
- Product organizations

## Success Criteria
The project succeeds when an engineering leader can understand how I would work before interviewing me.

## Philosophy
This is not a portfolio of documents. It is a realistic simulation of how Quality Assurance grows together with a software company.
